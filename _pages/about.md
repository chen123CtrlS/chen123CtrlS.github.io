Hi~ 😉 I am Xinhao Yao (姚鑫浩). Xinhao Yao is currently pursuing his Ph.D. at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China (RUC), under the supervision of [Prof.Yong Liu](https://gsai.ruc.edu.cn/liuyong). He earned his B.S. degree in artificial intelligence from the Renmin University of China in 2024. 

My current research interests are reasoning and machine learning, particularly the foundational theoretical analysis. I believe that `a good theory should not only
explain observations but also inform design`.
